Battery Components

The OR-TEC 12V / 7Ah AGM Battery is composed of high-quality materials engineered for durability, safety, and efficiency. Below is a breakdown of its core components:

🔋 Lead Plates

High-purity lead plates increase energy density and reduce corrosion, ensuring long service life.

🧪 Electrolyte (AGM)

Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) technology immobilizes the electrolyte, enhancing safety and performance.

🧱 ABS Plastic Casing

Rugged and flame-retardant outer casing provides structural integrity and impact resistance.

🛡️ Safety Valves

Built-in pressure relief valves prevent overpressure and ensure safe operation.

🔌 Terminals

Corrosion-resistant terminals ensure reliable electrical connections and easy installation.

🌡️ Separator

High-quality fiberglass separator prevents short circuits and enhances electrolyte distribution.

All components comply with international safety and environmental standards.
